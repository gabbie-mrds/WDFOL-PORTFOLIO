PROJECT - 2024


ROLE:

Graphic Designer

TOOLS:

Canva


This project is scenario-specific; we were to design a logo and a business card based on the business's identity.


I came up with this design since it was about Real Estate. I made the logo from scratch, and to signify that it is real estate, I used a roof shape at the top. For the business card, I made sure to use the color that I used for the logo to be consistent. Additionally, I used grainy texture to symbolize the fact that it was about a real estate developer and that every project starts from the ground.
